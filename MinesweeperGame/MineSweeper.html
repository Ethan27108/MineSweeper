Rules:

  • Click on a cell to reveal its content
  • Use right-click to flag a cell as a mine
  • Complete the game by flagging all mines

  • Power-Ups:
  • Spotter: For one click it will tell you the value of what you click on
  • Grid: For one click surroudning the click making a 3x3 area tells you how many mines are in the 3x3
  • Shield: For one click if you click on a mine will tell you and save you from death
